The real cost of no-shows

Every empty appointment slot costs your clinic money

Nigerian clinics lose an average of 3–5 appointments per day to no-shows and late cancellations. That's revenue you've already spent staff time to earn.

📵

Patients forget

No reminder system means patients simply forget appointments booked days or weeks ahead.

📋

Paper registers fail

Manual booking books get lost, double-booked, or can't be checked remotely by your team.

💸

Revenue walks out

A clinic with 20 appointments/day losing 4 to no-shows loses over ₦360,000/month at ₦3,000 per visit.

😓

Staff waste time

Receptionists spend hours calling patients manually — time better spent on in-clinic care.

What Vitar does

Everything your clinic needs, nothing it doesn't

Built specifically for Nigerian private clinics and specialist practices — not adapted from a Western product.

📅

Smart appointment scheduling

Let patients book online via your clinic's public booking page or QR code, or have staff book directly from the dashboard.

🔔

AI-powered reminders

Automatic SMS, WhatsApp, and email reminders timed to each patient's behaviour — not just 24 hours before.

🧠

No-show risk prediction

AI scores every appointment's no-show risk from 0–100% so your team can focus follow-up calls where they matter most.

👥

Patient management

Full patient records, visit history, and contact management — searchable in seconds, not buried in a register.

📊

Analytics dashboard

See your busiest days, highest-risk patients, revenue by month, and no-show trends — all in one screen.

🏥

QR code check-in

Print a QR code for your waiting room. Patients scan to confirm arrival — no receptionist needed for that step.
